What kind of Universities will you help us apply to?



Well we take pride in saying that we have helped students to reach the University that they desired.  More than 70% of our students land in a University that is either in the ambitious or profile category. Specifically, we have had students who wanted to go to


  1. Nothing less than the Ivy League Universities

  2. Top ranked schools for their specialization/ program

  3. Universities that would give them the maximum   aid

  4. Universities that would offer certain advantages – location, exposure, Industry etc...

Irrespective of their choices above, we have ensured that our students pursue their studies only at Accredited Universities. And in majority of the cases, we have ensured that their university is not only accredited by the country’s accrediting body but also by professional accrediting bodies like AACSB/AMBA/EQUIS, ABET etc.
