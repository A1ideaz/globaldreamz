What kind of aid will you secure for us?


Year after year, we have been able to consistently help students with a variety of funding options, which can be summarized as below:


  1. Tuition fee waivers: - we have always had a decent number of MBA and MS aspirants who have secured 100% aid, mostly as fellowships and tuition fee waivers.  Moreover, a substantial number of Global Dreamz students do get waivers between 25% and 70%. 

  2. Assistantships: - Understanding the breadth and depth of the program the University and the student‘s profile has helped us in securing assistantships like Graduate Assistantship, Research Assistantship and Teaching Assistantship. While RA was mostly awarded to MS Students, we have been consistently successful in getting GA’s and TA’s for BS, MS and MBA aspirants.

  3. On-Campus Jobs: - Working with Universities closely we have been able to identify Universities that are willing to offer on-campus jobs to students when they feel that a student’s profile may not qualify for Assistantships.  In many cases, the same has been instrumental in helping students defray some of their living expenses.

  4. University Guaranteed Loans: - Who doesn’t want to study in a scenario where in the initial cash out-flow is kept at the minimum and also secure a loan without any collateral or co-guarantor.  Many of our students year after year get admitted into universities wherein the student has to barely take care of the living expenses. 

Our research has identified that there are more than 100+ well reputed universities in the European countries wherein one can pursue their Masters at Costs that are substantial less than even the Indian MS programs.
