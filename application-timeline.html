Application Timeline


Students are generally curious about their preparedness and about timing their application. The chart below would help you identify the track that you are currently in OR the track that you may want to adopt in order to achieve your study abroad objectives. We at Global Dreamz would advise the students to take the ‘Ideal’ track as it’s more accommodative and has enough room for meeting out contingencies. However, if situation warrants, the student may also take the ‘Fast Track’ and be rest assured that we are there to assist you to the best our ability.
