Programs & Options


Your study abroad process becomes all the more attractive due to the myriad options that is available to you. However the choice is also the problem by itself! You need to take informed decisions and choose a program that is in line with your interests, provide enough career progression as well as help you achieve ‘wealth & fame’.

You could decide to work on programs that are

Traditional

Like the pure MS & Ph.D courses – which cater to your research interests and further your career in research/academics.

Career Oriented

Mostly known as ‘terminal degrees’ including courses like MBA, LLM, PSM etc., are your launch pads towards a great career right after the completion of your degree.

Inter-disciplinary

Offered under the traditional format / career oriented format, these interdisciplinary courses are a true mélange of various disciplines viz., Bio-Mechanics, Bio-informatics, Gerontology, etc., and are expected to equip you with a broader range of skill that the current employers demand.

Deciding a program type and choosing the right program that would catapult your career progression is the need of the hour, and when properly done your chances or becoming one of the ‘Young Achiever’ becomes a reality.

Example

A Computer Science undergraduate (B.E Computer Science/B.Tech /I.T etc.,) has to carefully choose amongst the following Master’s options available: MS.Computer Science, MS Computer Engineering MS. MIS (Management Information Systems), MS. ITM (Information & Technology Management), MS.Artificial Intelligence, MS.Robotics, MS.Software Engineering, MS.Human-Computer Interaction, MS.System Science (or even career-paths like) MS.Bioinformatics, MS.Financial Engineering, MBA etc.,

The diversity in program choices, as above are equally available for students from all streams…. electrical / mechanical / communications / fine arts / law / medicine etc. And the breadth of such programs are always on the rise as universities keep identifying and offer new degrees in tune with industrial demands.

Talk to us and we shall help you navigate through the same and help you applying to programs that would matter to you.
