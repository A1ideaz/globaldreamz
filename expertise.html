What’s your expertise in handling our profile?



Foot falls to Global Dreamz are basically through word of mouth.  This has basically challenged our ability to tune our services to cater to all kind of profiles that comes through references.  And we are glad to have accommodated them all and help them realize their dreams.

Apart from the bulk of the students who seek for admissions into regular MS or MBA programs,

we have had students with very specific request like: –

 

  1. pursue a Phd directly after their Bachelors
  2. physically challenged student seeking admissions abroad
  3. Student’s with 8+ years of experience seeking an admission into Masters Program.
  4. ‘GRE/GMAT phobiac’ students with deep rooted interest to pursue higher studies in USA/Canada
  5. securing admission for special programs like LLM, Supply Chain Management, Operation Management, Financial Engineering, Computer Graphics, Media Management, DPT, MFA, M-Design, Textile design and Merchandising,        Sustainable Resources...

 A Gist of the profile of the students who have enrolled with us is as follows:


  1. Competitive Profiles: Around 35% of the students posses a GMAT in excess of 700 or a GRE of 325+ with a high GPA of 3.5+.  The average work experience for the MBA aspirants is roughly 40 months. 

  2. Regular Profiles: Students with a GMAT in the range of 600- 700 or a GRE in the range of 300 – 325 with a GPA between 3.0 – 3.5 constitute 50% of our student base. 

  3. Unique Profiles: Every year close to 15% of our student’s posses a profile that’s quite unique – either they have a substantially low GRE/GMAT or huge no of second attempts (backlogs) or work experience that is in excess of 60 months. 

We are pretty confident that we would be able to handle your admission requirements to the hilt and help you reach the university that you desire to get into.  Talk to us to know more!
