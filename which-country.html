Which country will you help us reach?


During the Assessment phase of the application process we would discuss with you about your career aspirations, needs and affordability.  Further, we would also take a closer look at your program choice and your Post Qualification intentions.  Based on the outcome of the discussion, we would help you arrive at Country/Countries you should be applying to. 

In the past our students have applied to the following Countries:


  1. Americas – USA and Canada

  2. Europe – German, France, Italy, Spain, Sweden, Netherlands, Switzerland, UK, Ireland, Hungary, Belgium, Denmark

  3. Asia – Singapore, Hongkong, China, India, Philippines, Malaysia

  4. Australia and Newzealand

Our past doesn’t bind us and we are willing to look at other countries as & when options open up. The most important criteria for us would be the benefit that the student would gain by pursuing their studies in a particular Country.   Going by the rise of the BRICS Nations we are actively pursuing Universities in Brazil, Russia and South Africa and soon look forward to help students pursue their studies in these nations.
